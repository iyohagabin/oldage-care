Who are we?

Oldage Care is an non for profit organization that is geared towards providing aid for the senior citizens (Age 60/65 upwards)... Our long term goal is to build a full functional and well equipped elder home in each state of the federation Short term plan is payment Monthly Salary of 5k for Senior Citizen (age 80 and above) for life. Long run, we hope to start paying the salary from age 65 but for now. We are starting with age 80 and above. However we would provide foodstuff and other relief items to people from age 60 upwards.
